OUR PHILOSOPHY

The paramount role of EASTWOODS College is to empower our studentry, through education and training, with a solid foundation of knowledge, skills and moral values necessary in the attainment of their potential enabling them to be competitive, proactive and productive citizens of our country.



E-LEARNING

Education will continue even if there is a pandemic. nothing can stop us from teaching students, We are with you on your journey through this pandemic we face. This pandemic is not a reason for a student to stop studying using modern technologies we will teach for the sake of the students. With online classes, we don’t need to travel to a different city or commute long distances. We can stay where we are and keep our current job while we work toward improving our career with an online degree.









MODULAR LEARNING

We also know that some students have slow internet or no device available for Online class. Self learning can be quite useful for those who do not have the time to enroll in formal courses and degrees as well as for those who cannot travel to a specific location to educate themselves. Modular learning is effective In this approach the students learn at their own pace, they are feel free to learn in their own way. Parents have enough time to their children. They can provide assistance in teaching learning process.
